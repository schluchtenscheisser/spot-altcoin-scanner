# Future_Paid_Enhancements_Backlog (updated)

This backlog captures higher-cost extensions that improve performance and professionalism, typically requiring paid APIs or significant infrastructure.

## Paid / higher-cost data layers
- Derivatives context: Funding, Open Interest, liquidation/positioning indicators.
- On-chain analytics: flows, whales, exchange netflows, holders, smart money heuristics.
- High-quality event calendars: unlock schedules, listings/delistings, governance timelines, releases.
- News & sentiment at scale: broad coverage + low latency, social velocity/engagement.
- Cross-exchange market data: consolidated orderbooks/trades, dispersion/arbitrage metrics.
- Execution-grade microstructure: trade tape, aggressor, CVD, orderbook dynamics over time.
- “Real volume” / exchange quality scoring: wash-trade adjusted volume quality.
- Options / implied volatility surface / skew (if relevant for regime/tail risk).

## Higher-cost engineering / production
- Observability suite: provider SLAs, drift detection, alerting, lineage and dataset versioning.
- Portfolio/risk engine: factor models, vol targeting, scenario stress tests, exposure limits.
